# SOP-C010: SOP Quality Review Protocol

## Purpose
Provide systematic quality assessment criteria for evaluating SOPs to ensure they meet effectiveness, usability, and consistency standards.

## SOP Quality Assessment Process

### 1. **Structural Quality Review**
- **Format Compliance**: Verify SOP follows established format structure
- **Title Format**: Confirm title follows SOP-[Level][Number]: [Descriptive Name] pattern
- **Required Sections**: Ensure Purpose, Process Steps, Quality Gates, and Anti-Patterns sections exist
- **Section Organization**: Validate logical flow and clear section boundaries

### 2. **Content Quality Review**
- **Purpose Clarity**: Verify purpose statement clearly defines what the SOP accomplishes
- **Actionable Steps**: Confirm each step has clear, executable actions
- **Specificity**: Ensure guidance is specific rather than vague or generic
- **Completeness**: Validate process covers full workflow from start to finish
- **Success/Failure Criteria**: Verify clear outcomes defined for each major step

### 3. **Process Quality Review**
- **Quality Gates**: Confirm quality gates exist between all major step categories
- **Logical Flow**: Test logical progression and completeness of process steps
- **Decision Points**: Validate clear decision criteria and branching logic
- **Error Handling**: Confirm failure scenarios and recovery paths addressed
- **Testability**: Ensure process steps can be objectively validated

### 4. **Integration Quality Review**
- **Trigger Keywords**: Validate keywords align with intended use cases and discovery
- **Consistency**: Check terminology and format consistency with existing SOPs
- **Cross-References**: Verify appropriate references to other SOPs where relevant
- **Registry Integration**: Confirm SOP properly integrated in metadata registry

## Quality Assessment Checklist

### **Structural Quality Gates**
- [ ] Title follows SOP-[Level][Number]: [Name] format?
- [ ] Purpose section clearly defines SOP objective?
- [ ] Process steps organized with clear headings?
- [ ] Quality gates checklist included?
- [ ] Anti-patterns section identifies common mistakes?

### **Content Quality Gates**
- [ ] Each step has clear, executable actions?
- [ ] Guidance is specific rather than generic?
- [ ] Success and failure criteria defined for major steps?
- [ ] Process covers complete workflow?
- [ ] Content is actionable and testable?

### **Process Quality Gates**
- [ ] Quality gates exist between all major step categories?
- [ ] Logical flow tested and validated?
- [ ] Decision points have clear criteria?
- [ ] Error scenarios and recovery addressed?
- [ ] Process steps can be objectively validated?

### **Integration Quality Gates**
- [ ] Trigger keywords appropriate for intended use?
- [ ] Terminology consistent with existing SOPs?
- [ ] Cross-references accurate and helpful?
- [ ] Registry entry created with correct triggers?
- [ ] Documentation updated where needed?

## Quality Assessment Outcomes

### **Pass Criteria**
- All structural quality gates passed
- All content quality gates passed  
- All process quality gates passed
- All integration quality gates passed
- No critical deficiencies identified

### **Conditional Pass Criteria**
- Minor deficiencies identified but core functionality intact
- Specific improvement recommendations documented
- Timeline established for addressing deficiencies
- Re-assessment scheduled after improvements

### **Fail Criteria**
- Critical structural deficiencies (missing required sections)
- Major content issues (vague, untestable, incomplete)
- Process logic flaws (missing quality gates, illogical flow)
- Integration failures (wrong triggers, inconsistent terminology)

## Common Quality Issues

### **Anti-Patterns**
❌ Missing quality gates between major process steps
❌ Vague or untestable success criteria
❌ Inconsistent format or terminology
❌ Generic guidance that lacks specificity
❌ Incomplete process coverage
❌ Missing error handling or failure scenarios
❌ Poor trigger keyword selection
❌ Inconsistent cross-referencing

### **Excellence Indicators**
✅ Clear, actionable steps with specific examples
✅ Comprehensive quality gates and validation criteria
✅ Consistent terminology and format
✅ Complete process coverage including error scenarios
✅ Appropriate cross-references and integration
✅ Well-chosen trigger keywords for discovery
✅ Real-world tested and validated

## Post-Assessment Actions

### **For New SOPs**
- Document assessment results
- Address any deficiencies before integration
- Update working memory with testing requirements
- Schedule follow-up assessment after real-world use

### **For Existing SOPs**
- Document improvement recommendations
- Prioritize critical deficiencies for immediate attention  
- Schedule regular re-assessment cycles
- Track improvement trends over time

### **Assessment Documentation**
- Record assessment date and results
- Document specific deficiencies and recommendations
- Track resolution of identified issues
- Maintain assessment history for trend analysis
